Hangout Hunt Support

Questions, bugs, or account help — here's how to reach us and what to try first.

Common issues

  • Trouble logging in: use "Forgot password?" on the login screen to reset via a code sent to your email. If you don't receive it, check spam before contacting us.
  • Group session won't start or sync: make sure everyone has a stable connection — the lobby and swiping screens update in real time and need one.
  • Activity suggestions look off or don't load: this is usually a temporary issue with the suggestion service — try again in a moment, and let us know if it persists.
  • Want to delete your account: go to Account → Delete Account in the app. This is permanent and removes your profile and account immediately. See our Privacy Policy for details on what's deleted.

Contact us

For anything not covered above — bug reports, feedback, or account/data requests — email aarushparikh999@gmail.com. We'll get back to you as soon as we can.
